We see a connection between approximating antiderivatives and approximating areas.

A central theme of this course has been that we can often gain a better understanding of a function by looking at its derivative, and then working backwards. This has been our approach to max/min problems, curve sketching, linear approximation, and so on. So antiderivatives have really been important to us all along. We have a geometric interpretation of the derivative as the slope of a tangent line at a point. We have not yet found a geometric interpretation of antiderivatives.
